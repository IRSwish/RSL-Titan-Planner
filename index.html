Welcome to RSL Tools

Swish - RSL Tools is a collection of utilities designed to enhance your experience in Raid: Shadow Legends. Whether you’re a new player optimizing your first summons or a veteran tracking your legendary progress, this site gives you all the tools you need to go further.

Explore features like the Pity Counter, the Champion Database, and event calculators for Fusions, Tournaments and Clan Events. Each section is crafted to provide clear, precise, and customizable insights into your account progression.

Your data is stored locally in your browser — clearing your cache will reset your information. Enjoy the experience, and may the RNG gods smile upon your shards!
