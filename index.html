Welcome to RSL Tools

Swish - RSL Tools is a collection of utilities built to help you track and optimize your journey in Raid: Shadow Legends. Every feature is designed to make your experience smoother, clearer, and more rewarding — whether you’re preparing for events, checking fusions, or managing your in-game progress.

The goal is simple: provide a clean, efficient, and stylish way to stay on top of everything happening in the game. Each tool focuses on clarity and usability, with visuals that match the dark and vibrant spirit of Teleria.

Your data is stored locally in your browser — clearing your cache will reset your information.
Enjoy exploring, and may the RNG gods smile upon your shards!
